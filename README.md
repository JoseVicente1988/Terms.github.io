Privacy Policy for Laser Mirror Puzzle

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Laser Mirror Puzzle is a puzzle game developed by Jose Vicente Vicedo Silla.

This game does not collect, store, or share personal information from users.

The game may store basic local progress data on the user's device, such as completed levels, settings, or gameplay progress. This data is stored locally and is not sent to any external server.

Laser Mirror Puzzle does not require account registration, does not collect names, email addresses, location data, contacts, photos, or any sensitive personal information.

If future versions of the game include online features, advertising, analytics, leaderboards, or user accounts, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Children's Privacy

Laser Mirror Puzzle is suitable for general audiences. The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

This beta version does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external tracking services.
